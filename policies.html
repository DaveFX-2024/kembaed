Bureau Policies & Regulations

The comprehensive legal and operational guidelines governing education within the Kembata Zone.

Academic Excellence Standards

To ensure uniform educational quality, all regional schools must comply with the 2026 Academic Integrity framework. This policy outlines the mandatory hours of instruction, laboratory requirements, and teacher-to-student ratios.

Instructional Hours

Minimum of 1,100 contact hours per academic year for secondary schools.

Classroom Density

Standardized at 45 students per classroom to ensure effective pedagogy.

Staff Code of Conduct

This policy defines the professional expectations for all educators and administrative staff within the Bureau's jurisdiction.

  • Mandatory punctuality: Arrival 15 minutes before the first bell.
  • Annual professional development: 40 hours of certified training required.
  • Ethical engagement: Zero tolerance for discriminatory behavior.
